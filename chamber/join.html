Membership Levels
Non Profit Membership Level

The non profit membership level is the least level a member can subscribe to.
Subscription fee is $100.
Members of this level would be entitled to special events
Bronze Membership Level

The Bronze membership level is the second level a member can subscribe to.
Subscription fee is $200.
Members of this level would be entitled to special events and opportunity to advertise on our home page.
Silver Membership Level

The Silver membership level is the third level a member can subscribe to.
Subscription fee is $300.
Members of this level would be entitled to special events and opportunity to advertise on our home page. In addition, members would also have the opportunity to undergo special training.
Gold Membership Level

The Gold membership level is the highest level a member can subscribe to.
Subscription fee is $400.
Members of this level would be entitled to special events and opportunity to advertise on our home page. In addition, members would also have the opportunity to undergo special training as well as have access to special event discounts
